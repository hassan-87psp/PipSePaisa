💡 How to Use These Zones
🟢 Demand Zone (BUY)

Jab price demand zone tak aaye, BUY ka mauka. Stop loss zone ke thora niche, target next supply zone.

🔴 Supply Zone (SELL)

Jab price supply zone tak chare, SELL ka mauka. Stop loss zone ke thora upar, target next demand zone.

⚖️ Important Tips

Higher timeframe (Daily/4H) zones zyada strong hote hain. Confirmation candle ka wait karein, andha BUY/SELL na karein.
